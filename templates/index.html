Machine Learning Models for Predicting Single-Value Thermodynamic Properties of Chemical Compounds

Single-value thermodynamic properties are properties that are not temperature or pressure dependent such as normal boiling point, compressibility factor, and others. These models were trained on a dataset of 22,968 compounds that was obtained from ThermoDataEngine software from the National Institute of Standards and Technology of the U.S. Department of Commerce.

It predicts values of:

  1. Normal boiling point
  2. Enthalpy of Formation (Gaseous state)
  3. Acentric factor
  4. Critical compressibility factor
